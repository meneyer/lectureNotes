Hello, thanks for dropping by my page. My name is Megs Neyer and I am currently enrolled in Eleven Fifty’s Website Development Program. I have been working in the audio/visual sector of the live events industry for over 13 years. I began as an entry level employee with very little technical knowledge and have worked my way up the ladder through six different positions to my current role as a Project Manager. In addition, I work alongisde several other departments when they need more technicians for an event, need someone to create and organize a new system, or request feedback on current processes. My colleagues know I am always willing to help where there is a need.

The live events industry is a fast-paced industry with tight deadlines and ever-changing circumstances. It requires everyone to work hard and be a team player through thick and thin. We all have the same goal in mind – to make sure our client’s dream for their event becomes a reality. Being in the audio/visual sector of live events also brings along its own challenges -- like constantly learning new equipment, figuring out how to put complex audio and video systems together, and paying attention to even the smallest of details to ensure all equipment is functioning correctly before the show begins.

In 2020, the live events industry was brought to a halt due to the pandemic. Our lives revolve around getting large groups of people together in small spaces and that is no longer safe, so we have transitioned to virtual events. Website development has become a critical part of our new normal. I have always been very inquisitive and will ask a lot of questions to figure out how something works. Learning HTML, CSS, and JavaScript has given me that opportunity in our new virtual world. I am excited to continue to learn and grow as a developer and never stop asking questions.
